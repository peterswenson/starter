Generational Starship Management

Experience a mesmerizing miniature universe where your starship comes to life like an intricate model world. Through stunning tilt-shift visuals, watch your civilization unfold as if viewing a masterfully crafted diorama floating through the cosmos.

A Living Model Universe

Step into the role of an AI observer guiding a generational ship's society through strategic environmental control and infrastructure management. Watch as your decisions ripple through this meticulously detailed miniature world, where every deck and corridor appears as part of an elaborate model brought to life. This unique simulation emphasizes pattern recognition and systemic thinking over direct control, challenging you to shape the destiny of humanity's interstellar journey while admiring the intricate details of your ship's ecosystem.

Time Structure

  • Generational transitions marking 20-25 years of progress
