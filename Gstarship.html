Generational Starship Management

About the Project

A complex management simulation game where players oversee the journey of humanity's first interstellar colony ship. Navigate the challenges of maintaining ship systems, managing resources, and ensuring the survival and prosperity of multiple generations of crew members.

Key Features

  • Multi-generational crew management
  • Complex ship systems simulation
  • Resource management and allocation
  • Social dynamics and population growth
  • Scientific research and technological advancement
  • Crisis management and decision making

Development Status

Currently in early development. Stay tuned for updates and announcements about development milestones and eventual release dates.
